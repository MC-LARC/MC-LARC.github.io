Input Image Explanation

  • In the input, you should see...two pixels of different colors on the upper or lower rim, or on the right or left rim.

MC-LARC options

To make the output, you have to...illustrate the input grid. Draw cross lines from y color dots till it reaches the immediate lines of x color. The touch point should be painted with the y color. If the y color dot is between y color lines, make a horizontal line of y color passing through the y color dot and connecting the x color lines. keep a boundary around every y color dot (touch point) on the x color line with an x color triangle. It needs to be a triangle of x color with its tip of y color on the x color line. Done.

Option 1

To make the output, you have to...mark the input grid. Create parallel lines from the dots of y color to touch the nearest squares of x color. The point of touch should be the y color. If the dot of y color is between 2 lines of y color you should make one lines of y color bypassing the dot of y color and bypassing the lines of x colors. Encircle each dot of y color (point of touch) on the line of x color with a circle of x color. It should be a square of x color with the center of x color on the line of x color. Done.

Option 2

To make the output, you have to...duplicate the given grid. Draw intersecting lines from the squares of y color to get closer to the nearby lines of x color. The touching point has to be the y color. If the y color square is in between two y color lines, create a line of y color passing through the y color square and intersecting the lines of x colors. Surround the y color square (point of touch) where the x color line is with a rectangle of x color. it has to be a rectangle of x color with the core of y color on the x color line. Done.

Option 3

To make the output, you have to...copy the input grid. Make perpendicular lines from the dots of y color to touch the closest lines of x color. The point of touch should be the y color. If the dot of y color is between 2 lines of y color you should make one lines of y color going through the dot of y color and connecting the lines of x colors. Surround each dot of y color (point of touch) on the line of x color with a square of x color. It should be a square of x color with the center of y color on the line of x color. Done.

Option 4

To make the output, you have to...copy the input template. Draw diagonal lines from the dots of y color touching the immediate near lines of x color. The touching area needs to be of y color. In case the y color dot is between two lines of same color, construct a single y color line crossing the y color dot and linking the x color lines. Border every y color dot (point of contact) on the x color line with a polygon of x color. It should form a polygon of x color with the centre point of y color on the x color line. Done.
