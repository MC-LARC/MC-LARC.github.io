MC-LARC options

To make the output, you have to maintain all the colors in their original positions and not alter any of them.

Option 1

To make the output, you have to swap the inner most color with the outer most colors and proceed similarly with every color until all have been changed. In other words, reverse the order of the colors.

Option 2

To make the output, you have to change every third color to a completely new color that wasn’t used before.

Option 3

To make the output, you have to reverse the order of the rows while preserving the sequence of the colors.

Option 4

To make the output, you have to swap the colors in random order until the grid is completely filled.
