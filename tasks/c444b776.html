Input Image Explanation

  • In the input, you should see...a black grid divided into sections by one or some lines. And there are random color pixels in only one section.



MC-LARC options

  • To make the output, you have to copy the pattern in one region to any other region.

  • To make the output, you have to exchange the positions of colors between adjacent regions.

  • To make the output, you have to relocate the pattern in one section to a corresponding section.

  • To make the output, you have to delete the colors in each region symmetrically.

  • To make the output, you have to eliminate the colors from all regions.
