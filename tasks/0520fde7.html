MC-LARC options

To make the output, you have to find the input grid's colors on the upper and lower parts. Color the output grid for each non-colored square seen on both parts, using orange for the new 3x3.

Option 1

To make the output, you have to look at both the left and right parts of the input grid. You will notice that the left and right parts are 3x3. For each square that is colored on both the left and right parts, color the output grid with red on the new 3x3.

Option 2

Option 3

To make the output, you have to look at both the left and right parts of the input grid. You will notice that the left and right parts are 3x3. For each square that is not colored on either the left or right parts, erase the output grid with black on the new 3x3.

Option 4
